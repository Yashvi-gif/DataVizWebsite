Australia energy consumption by Fuel type, from 1974-2020

Australian Energy Statistics

the authoritative and official source of energy statistics for Australia

Graph Analysis

In this graph, on our x axis we have the Years and on our y axis we have the Energy consumption in Petajoules. The mark type is area and the channel type is Hue. We have a legend on the right which precisely defines which colour represents which energy. Stacked area chart here is allowing us to visualize the trend of usage of different energy types over the years and contribution of each to the overall energy usage; denoted by the height of the coloured area. We observe that the usage of Coal has reduced gradually over the years, while there is no noticeable change in the usage of oil. Gas and renewable energy sources have seen a slow yet gradual increase in their consumption over the years.
