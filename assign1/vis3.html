Graph Analysis

Using this visualisation, we can infer about the contribution level different countries to the Carbon emissions (CO2) into the environment. We can derive information about the countries which are polluting the environment more than the others and where the authorities can take the necessary steps to achieve our goal. Moreover, relevant steps can be taken by the government to promote ways to reduce these emissions. The visualisation can also be used to by various environmentalists and researchers for research purposes.

The attributes of the Scatter Plot visualization are: Energy consumption, CO2 emission levels, Different countries. We are fetching the dataset to create visualization from the csv file. Both energy consumption and CO2 emission level attributes are ratio. We intend to find the answers to the following questions from our visualisation:
For the same energy consumption levels, which countries have more CO2 emission levels and thus harming the environment?
Are there any countries which have unusually high CO2 emission level? If yes, which continent does it belong to?
Comparing any two given countries based on CO2 emission level/energy consumption levels.
